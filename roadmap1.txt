Risk Register and Mitigation Plan

Risk: dependency drift breaks scripts or CI.
Mitigation: periodic lock updates, CI on every PR, reproducible env notes.

Risk: retrieval quality regressions go unnoticed.
Mitigation: baseline retrieval metrics and regression thresholds.

Risk: provider misconfiguration causes runtime failures.
Mitigation: startup validation, clear env docs, failure-path tests.

Risk: onboarding friction for learners.
Mitigation: stepwise README learning paths and verified copy-paste commands.
